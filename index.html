Bonjour and Welcome to the City of Lights!

Ah Paris! Oui Oui!

This beautiful city is the capital and the heart of France. Many people travel to this place throughout the year. The best season to visit is in the summer time, where the weather is gorgeous. In the summer time, it is especially nice to enjoy all the outdoor activities and sceneries all around Paris and even throughout France itself. Come bring your loved ones, family and friends along to experience the city of lights and love. Let's go! Allons-y!

Where to stay?

Where to stay?

There are many places where one can stay at such as hotels, airbnb, hostels etc. Transportation is also easily accessible with the buses, metros, taxis and even within walking distances within the vicinity.

Lots of hotels offer delicious continental breakfast. No need to worry about waking up early to find something to eat. It will be right there waiting for you to enjoy before you venture out.

Where to explore?

Where to explore?

There are so many attractions, sites to see and places to eat in this lovely city you can't go wrong! One of the favorites are definitely the Eiffel Tower, The Louvre Museum, The Palace of Versaille and Notre Dame.

For your convenenience you can take the metro, bus, or taxi to avoid finding parking.
